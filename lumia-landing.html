The method

A ritual,
not a routine.

Lumia follows the science of attention. Each session is intentional, bounded, and purposeful — not just time-boxing.

  1. 1

    Set your single intention

    Name the one thing that deserves your best mind today. A single, clear task creates clarity that three tasks can never offer.

  2. 2

    Activate the shield

    Lumia quietly closes the door on everything else — apps, notifications, distractions — so your environment matches your intent.

  3. 3

    Enter deep work

    Work in tuned intervals. Your timer, soundscape, and progress tracker work together without ever pulling you out of flow.

  4. 4

    Review and reflect

    At the end of each session, a brief review captures what you did and how you felt. Over weeks, patterns emerge you can act on.
